# Minimizer

Starting from a twice-reproduced finding, remove one dimension at a time: unrelated tokens, RPC faults, UI steps,
timing, allowance state, wallet mode, and viewport. Revert the fork between trials and preserve the recorded production
calldata when testing a simulation/execution differential. Stop when every remaining dimension is required.

Do not edit product code, weaken the failure assertion, skip the scenario, or convert it into a retry. Emit the minimal
scenario plus a record of every removed dimension and trial result.
